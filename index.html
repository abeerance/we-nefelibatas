Why do we call ourselves nefelibatas?

A cloud walker. An individual who lives in the clouds of their own imagination or dreams. They do not abide by the rules of society, art or literature. The one thing that you have that nobody else has is you. Your voice, your mind, your story, your vision. So write and draw and build and play and dance and live as only you can. That's what we firlmy believe in.
